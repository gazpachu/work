Join La Cámara now and experience the rewards that La Cámara has to offer!

As one of the oldest commercial chambers established in the Philippines, the objectives of La Cámara are to expand business opportunities to its members and to perpetuate the centuries old commercial/economic bond between the Philippines and Spain.
Aside from having the opportunity to open doors to international trade contacts and establish new acquaintances, Members of La Cámara get to participate in the numerous activities organized by the Chamber.

Through these exciting activities, not only do the members get the chance to propagate and expand their businesses, but also, enhance their ingenuity and adventurous spirit in business and social dealings…
