Newsletter

El Galeón is the official newsletter of the Spanish Chamber of Commerce in the Philippines. Its name, vessels used both for war and commercial purposes, represents the commercial venture of Spain to the Philippines and the economic ties between the two countries.

Designed to benefit its members, the newsletter contains economic information, commercial offers and inquiries, activities undertaken by the Chamber and other interesting and relevant articles.
